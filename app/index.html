About Me

Short intro to me

I have always had a passion for building things. This can be seen from my hobbies and previous work experience. I enjoy taking a project from concept to finished product. When I am not behind a computer screen I enjoy attending automotive events.

My background was in rebuilding large-frame generators for multinational power generation companies. Prior to that I was a machinist station aboard the USS Emory S. Land in the Navy.

Before attending The Iron Yard Academy I was majoring in mechanical engineering at the University of Wisconsin- Milwaukee. I also owned and operated an automotive repair business with a focus on european cars.

Now that I have completed the intense front end engineering course at The Iron Yard I am seeking employment with a fun, product driven company.
